To Use: write-good

I maintain write-good, a project by my friend Brian Ford. It is a naive linter for English prose. This week, @vorror added and I merged E-Prime mode, which allows you to lint to all uses of the verb to be in your text, in case you're trying to eschew copulas to make your writing a bit more evocative. Kind of a fun little project.
